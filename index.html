Our Mission

LIFE Alliance stands as the home of the international community on the Blair Server where English, Spanish, French, Italian, and more players come together under one banner. We are more than just an alliance: we are a family built on friendship, teamwork, and the will to keep Blair diverse and alive.

Our mission is to create a space where every guild has a voice, every member has a role, and every fight is an opportunity to grow stronger together. Whether in PvP, PvM, or AvA, LIFE Alliance is about more than winning it's about belonging.

Your guild deserves more than just an alliance.

It deserves a home. It deserves LIFE.

Solum qui nobiscum steterunt, nobiscum stabunt.

Our Vision

We strive to:

  • Keep the international spirit alive in a server dominated by single-language groups.
  • Foster a community where activity, fairness, and respect are the foundations.
  • Compete in PvP, PvM, and AvA not only to win, but to show that diversity is strength.
  • Create an environment where every guild can grow, every leader can contribute, and every player can feel proud to wear the LIFE tag.

Alliance Phases

Phase 1 – Foundation

Focus: Stabilize & Engage
  • Strengthen leadership presence (introduce the leadership team clearly)
  • Increase activity through consistent events (lotteries, PvM/PvP, features)
  • Encourage guild leaders to show visibility in Discord and game
  • Build identity: "LIFE = International Home of Blair"
Goal: Reignite activity and stabilize at 100–120 peak actives
In Progress

Phase 2 – Expansion

Focus: Recruitment & Growth
  • Attract new guilds with bilingual recruitment (ENG/SPA/FR)
  • Form soft partnerships with mid size guilds
  • Promote LIFE's international uniqueness across forums and Discords
  • Use big events (like your PvP/PvM with 55mk prize) as recruitment showcases
Goal: Grow to 5k+ members across all guilds, visible as Blair's international hub
Upcoming

Phase 3 – Coordination

Focus: Training & Structure
  • Form strike teams (8–16 Teams) that can be reliably mobilized
  • Run mock AvAs and practice drills with leadership callers
  • Establish clear communication channels (Discord squads, AvA voice rooms)
  • Rotate leadership duties (events, recruitment, PvP organization)
Goal: 250 reliable fighters, 3 trained squads, council-led structure working smoothly
Upcoming

Phase 4 – AvA Relaunch

Focus: Visibility & War Readiness
  • Launch a hype campaign ("LIFE STRIKES BACK")
  • Organize 2 final mock AvAs with mandatory participation
  • Relaunch with a major AvA attempt (aim for 400–500 fighters)
  • Publicly recap the war, even if we lose → visibility builds momentum
Goal: Successful return to AvA, LIFE recognized as a serious force
Upcoming

Phase 5 – Legacy

Focus: Sustain & Evolve
  • Establish a cadence (1 AvA every 2–3 weeks)
  • Mentor smaller guilds to secure loyalty and pipeline growth
  • Celebrate community with monthly awards (best fighter, recruiter, etc.)
  • Keep Discord active with ongoing mini events (lotteries, Easter eggs, debate)
Goal: LIFE becomes the long term international alliance of Blair, known for both community and competitiveness
